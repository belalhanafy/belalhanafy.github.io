Sharm El-Sheikh Restaurants



1/On Deck Restaurant























On Deck Restaurant, a floating restaurant in Sharm El Sheikh, offers a truly unique dining experience with a breathtaking view of Naama Bay and a world of diverse marine life in the Red Sea. Guests here are invited to enjoy a varied menu of dishes with innovative cocktails in an elegant atmosphere with service amazing. On Deck Restaurant serves Mediterranean dishes, such as grilled lamb chops, high-quality seafood dishes, including lobster and shrimp and a variety of assorted fish either fried or grilled with exquisite Egyptian salads.


2/Mojo Restaurant























it is located in Naama Bay directly in front of the Lido Hotel.. It is located in front of the sea. You can sit inside and enjoy its decorations and artistic paintings that adorn it.. Or sit on the terrace overlooking the sea.. Everyone who sat there said that he feels like he is in the American Miami, this hotel serves a salad Sea with lemon.. Spanish chocolate.. and many exotic dishes from different countries, especially from Europe and the Middle East.

work time From 12 AM to 12 PM



3/Fayrouz Restaurant























one of the best restaurants in Sharm El-Sheikh that offers many Egyptian and Lebanese light meals that are distinguished by their delicious taste, and the kibbeh dish is one of the most famous dishes in the place.

work time 11 AM-1 PM

4/The Blue Fontaine























this restaurant is one of the best restaurants in Sharm El-Sheikh, as it offers its visitors a mixture of British and French cuisine with Mediterranean dishes, so it is an ideal place for gourmet food lovers from visiting the city, especially as you will find here what you are looking for from dishes whatever your taste, as well as About that the restaurant attracts its guests with wonderful oriental shows in the evening.


5/Octopus Seafood Restaurant























one of the best restaurants that offer Sea Food in Sharm El Sheikh. It is located in Naama Bay. The tourist says that it offers a plate of various salads and cheeses with the same quality as London restaurants.

work time From 10 a.m. - 10 p.m.



6/Casa Miya






















avery nice restaurant in Naama Bay.. It is in the Bedouin style, and you can call it and deliver everyone to the hotel or any place you want. Enter his website.. you will find his number and the dishes he serves, in addition to many pictures. Site Builder

work time From 6 pm - 1 am


7/Hard Rock Restaurant






















one of the best Sharm El-Sheikh restaurants that offers many dishes of the American cuisine with many fast food dishes and drinks, and the steak dish is one of the restaurant's best dishes.

times of work: Daily from 12:00 AM - 04:00 AM


8/Fares Restaurant






















the best restaurant in Sharm El Sheikh offers a range of seafood dishes that are cooked in a distinctive Egyptian way, with many dishes of hot and cold appetizers, and the crab dish is one of the best dishes in the place.

times of work: Daily from 12:00 AM - 01:00 AM
